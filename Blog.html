Essential Concepts in Web Development

Web development today emphasizes structure, design, and responsiveness. Key concepts like HTML5 semantic elements, responsive design, Flexbox, CSS specificity, and gradients are crucial for building efficient and visually engaging websites.

Semantic elements in HTML5 are tags that add meaning to the structure of a webpage, making it more understandable for both developers and browsers. They enhance accessibility, SEO, and readability by defining specific sections of a webpage. For instance, elements like "header", "main", and "footer" each serve a unique purpose. "header" generally includes introductory content or navigation links, "main" represents the central content of the page, and "footer" often holds author information or legal notices.
